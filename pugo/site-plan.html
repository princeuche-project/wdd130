Overview

Purpose

The PUGO clothing and shoe boutique mission is to offer quality, name brand clothes, shoes and accessories in an assortment of sizes and styles to accommodate the chic in every woman, man and child.

Audience

PUGO clothing and shoes Boutique will carry only top of the line quality in women's shoes, clothes, and accessories. Our selection will range from the basics of confortable flats and sandals to the trendy style of stiletto shoes and boots. We will be purchasing through sales representative and manufacturers in a variety of sizes, colors, and styles to fit our customers base. The greatest percentage , of merchandise will be in shoes , followed by clothes and accessories. Pugo clothing and shoes is organized as a sole proprietorship in the name of Prince Uchenna Godstime Owuamanam. Pugo will be located at Alaba Internatinal market Lagos plaza temporarily, until it is vast enough to expand into store of it own, located in Eastern and Southen Nigeria. The hours of operation will be Monday – Friday 8 a.m. – 6 p.m., Saturday 10 a.m. – 8 p.m., and closed on Sundays. Customers will be able to purchase directly from our website and VIP Showcase Events. There will be extended special hours designated during Christmas/festive holiday shopping season. All merchandise will be purchased according to the company’s mission and customer focus of shoes ranging from sizes 5-13, clothes and accessories. Management will rely on customer feedback, suggestions, and sales reports to introduce or eliminate certain brands, styles and sizes.

The concept

Recognizing that the market for quality shoes in extended sizes is quite competitive using the larger shoe retailers, PUGO clothing and shoe boutique will minimize the competition by targeting specific niches, generally focusing on other retail stores whose prices are too expensive, lack quality and are not “shoe and clothe size efficient”. PUGO clothing and shoe boutique has three keys to success. The first is establishing a high quality, high value relationship with its vendors and customers. The second key is to ensure reliable and timely delivery of their products. The last key to success is reliable administration of the organization. This boutique business plan is intended to provide the structure to make sure this last key to success is realized.

The Market

PUGO clothing and shoe boutique will grow at 3% and there are many potential customers now, patiently awaiting the start of business. PUGO clothing and shoe boutique will differ from other retail clothing and shoe stores because we will always be less expensive than the competition, but we will still maintain the high quality of our shoes.

Also, we will offer a sales promotion on a monthly basis, such as:

  • Buy one, get one half price
  • Buy one, get one free half off on shoes from the previous season

PUGO clothing and shoe boutique Naira (for every N50,000 purchase, the consumer receives an additional 20% off of their next purchase) In the future, PUGO clothing and shoe boutique will design its own, custom-made shoes and clothes for women, men and children.

Objectives

The objectives of PUGO clothing and shoe boutique are:
  • To market trendy, clothes and elegant shoes, ranging from sizes 5-13, that will cater to the needs of the everyday woman.
  • To receive a 50% profit margin within the first year.
  • To provide customer service, measured through new and repeat business (our goal is that 50% of our customers will return within 6 months for an additional purchase) and multiple sales (our goal is that 30% of our non-running and 60% of our running clothes and shoe sales are accompanied by an additional purchase).
  • To achieve a net profit of N2,000,000.00 by year two and N6,000,000 by year five.

Mission

The PUGO clothing and shoe boutique mission is to offer quality, name brand clothes, shoes and accessories in an assortment of sizes and styles to accommodate the chic in every woman, man and child.

Keys to Success

In order to succeed in the women’s shoe industry PUGO clothing and shoe boutique must:
  • Carry an assortment of shoes sizes and style to fit the personalities of any woman.
  • Provide customers with top-notch personalized customer service in an atmosphere of hospitality
  • Advertise and promote in all areas so that our customer base will learn more about
    PUGO clothing and shoe boutique
  • Continuously review our inventory and sales and adjust our inventory levels accordingly.
  • Most importantly, always stay on top of the latest trends in the style of women’s clothes and shoes.
  • Set up as a home-based business, with plans for expansion into a store/boutique in the near future. This will be the most cost effective arrangement, yet provide the requisite amount of service and support.
