What is River?

The river is a natural stream of flowing water with a significant volume of freshwater. Rivers are pratically found everywhere on the land, and every continent. They start from source or headwater areas such as springs, glaciesr, and flow in meandering paths at different speeds . finally, they discharge into a larger river or lakes or most likely into oceans. River are the main channels of the drainage system. Some river flow seasonally or when it is wet. It may cover just kilometers or more.

Fun For Sport Activities

River are also used for various fun and sports-related activities like swimming, paragliding, boating, water sking, kayaking, canoeing among others. Rivers are also used for people for fishing . People often queue up on the bank of a river for fish catching and then enjoy doing the barbeque. various species need freshwater to quench their thirst. River thus become a great source to watch exotic species feed on animals that live in the rivers and birds diving to catch fish.
